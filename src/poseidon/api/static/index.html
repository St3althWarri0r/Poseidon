Active broker

Poseidon is non-custodial: your money stays at your brokerage and trades execute there through its official API. Activating a LIVE account while the mode is Autonomous drops it to Approval so a mis-click can't auto-trade real money; otherwise the operating mode is unchanged — a live account in research mode still cannot trade.
